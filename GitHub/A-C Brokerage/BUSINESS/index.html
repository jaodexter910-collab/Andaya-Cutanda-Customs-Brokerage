About Us

Andaya–Cutanda Customs Brokerage — Where Speed, Accuracy, and Trust Define Every Clearance.

In today's fast-moving trade environment, every second counts—and so does every detail. At Andaya–Cutanda Customs Brokerage, we position ourselves as your reliable customs specialist, committed to delivering seamless, efficient, and fully compliant customs clearance services.

We understand that customs procedures can be complex, time-sensitive, and critical to your operations. That's why our team is dedicated to managing every stage of the clearance process with precision—from documentation review and tariff classification to duty computation and regulatory compliance. Our goal is simple: to eliminate delays, reduce risks, and ensure your shipments move without interruption.

With a strong focus on professionalism and accountability, we provide:

  • Fast and efficient processing to minimize clearance time
  • Accurate documentation and compliance with all customs regulations
  • Transparent coordination and updates throughout the process
  • Client-focused solutions tailored to your specific import and export needs

At Andaya–Cutanda Customs Brokerage, we don't just clear shipments—we build lasting partnerships by delivering dependable service you can trust. We take pride in being proactive, detail-oriented, and results-driven, ensuring that your cargo reaches its destination smoothly and on time.

Because your business deserves a customs partner that prioritizes excellence at every step.
